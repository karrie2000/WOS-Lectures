Jinja's Favorite Tricks

Take a Bow

Jinja loves attention! Watch her take a bow to the delight of all in attendance.

No applause, please - just treats!

Middle

Got a treat? Hold it in front of you between your hands and say, “middle!”

She'll circle around between your legs and sit. Don't forget that treat!

Stick 'Em Up

Jinja's been working hard on her new trick. Give her the double finger guns and say, “middle!”

She'll put up both paws high in the air! Mmm... tasty treat time!
